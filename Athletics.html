2014: First-team all-UAA at linebacker ... Second on the squad and sixth in the UAA in tackles (79) ... Made season-high 12 tackles and added 16-yard sack in 59-19 win vs. Hendrix (10/11) ... Recorded second career interception in 47-19 win vs. Alfred State (11/8) ... UAA Defensive Athlete of the Week on Oct. 13 and Nov. 3 … Beyond Sports Network Third-Team Preseason All-American … Most Inspirational Player Award ... Team captain.

2013: First-team all-UAA … UAA Defensive Athlete of the Week on Sept. 30 and Oct. 28 … Started 10 of 11 games at linebacker and led team in tackles for loss (14) and sacks (4) and was second in tackles (73) … First in the UAA in tackles for loss (14), third in fumbles forced (3), fourth in sacks (4) and 10th in tackles (73) … Made season-high 12 tackles in 17-10 NCAA First Round loss at No. 20 Franklin … Had 11 tackles, two tackles for loss and a sack in 17-7 loss at No. 9 Whitewater (9/7) … WUSTL Defensive Player of the Game vs. Centre.

2012: First-team all-UAA … Led the team and ranked seventh in the UAA with 70 tackles … Added five pass break-ups and 3.5 tackles for loss … Recorded career-high 16 tackles and two tackles for loss at Rhodes (9/8) … UAA Defensive Athlete of the Week on Sept. 10.

2011: Played on the junior varsity … Saw action in two varsity games making two tackles.
